  • Raging bushfires in australia from september 2019 to march 2020

    September in 2019 to march 2020 was a period of some of the worst bushfires australia had ever seen. Throughout areas of New south wales and victoria fires raged for months burning around 18.6 million hectares of land. They also destroyed more 5900 buildings and killed atleast 34 people. Nearly 3 billion terrestrial vertebrates alone were affected and some endangered species were believed to be driven to extinction.
